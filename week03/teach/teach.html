Properties:
Properties are the values that are associated an object.

"last name", and the "gender".

Instantiation and differences

Instantiation is a word that just means "creating the object".
The important thing to note that's different between the two methods, is how things are created.
In the first method, everything is created at the one time in a variable. In the second method,
the object is first created, using the new operator, then the properties are created afterwards.
